Morgana, a 31 years old artist, established musician shows to Ireland how is to fight in an expensive city. Moreover, she explores the difficulties of making ends meet in an tough industry.
After the break up with her anterior group, she decides pursuing her dreams as an artist. Her exploration goes through deep emotional, soulful with electro-pop beats. The ways to express deepness in her lyrics catalog her as an Irish protagnist of the music revelation of this year.
Morgana discover herself as a solo artist after being confronted with the choices of her friends. Which were moving to buy houses and settle down into the normality of a formal world. That was not her case, she decides to go after what she believes, a difficult path but entertained for sure.
'Im Not Going Anywhere' an anthem of decision and determination showing to the public that there is going to be more Morgana for long.
