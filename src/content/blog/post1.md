The Morgana's new single 'Nothing Kills A Party Like A Song' is already out on YouTube and Spotify for streaming.
Her lyrics describe moments her fighting against social anxiety and how is to live under a spotlight on the scenario. This shows her human and emotional side, something that artists avoid for being commercial and sales oriented.
This single is just one of her many releases, which also talk about the music industry and the pressure of aging and success.
Coming from her previous duo, the ex Saint Sister, made her feel that she is in the correct path. She also mentions that singing definetly is something that will last for her. Being in the industry is tough but she inspiration is something that does not lack at this moment.
Providing passion in her lyrics and rythm transformed her world and everything she does. Her new single 'Nothing Kills A Party Like A Song' demonstrates that.
